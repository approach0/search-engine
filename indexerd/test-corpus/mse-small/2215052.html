My solution:

[imath]\lim_{x\to-1\\y=k(x^2+1)+1}\frac{4x-(k(x^2+1)+1)+5}{(1+2x+(k(x^2+1)+1))^2}=\lim_{x\to-1}\frac{4x-kx^2-k+4}{(2x+kx^2+k+2)^2}=\frac{-4-k-k+4}{(-2+k+k+2)^2}=\frac{-2k}{4k^2}[/imath]

To calculate variables with substitution it must be that on that particular pat you approach the wanted point... For example, take [imath]\;y=k(x+1)+1\;[/imath] (observe that [imath]\;x\to-1\;\implies\;y\to1\;[/imath], as required), so the limit is
