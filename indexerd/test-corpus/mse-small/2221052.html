Calculating percentage change from loss and gain

A bank makes a loss of [imath]1,000,000[/imath] in [imath]2016[/imath]. In [imath]2017[/imath] it makes a profit of [imath]1,000,000.[/imath] Is it possible to calculate the increase in percentage profit from [imath]2016[/imath] to [imath]2017[/imath]

It is not possible if you don't know the capital of the bank in 2016.

The 1,000,000 gain in 2017 cancels the 1,000,000 loss in 2016, putting the total amount of money in the bank back to where it started. So the percent increase in profit is zero.
