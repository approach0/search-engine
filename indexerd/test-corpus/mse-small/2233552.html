@vrugtehagel yes, all are positive.

Let [imath]\{a,b,c,d\}=\{x,y,z,t\}[/imath], where [imath]x\geq y\geq z\geq t[/imath].
Hence, by Rearrengement and AM-GM we obtain:
[imath]\frac{a}{b}+\frac{b}{c}+\frac{c}{d}+\frac{d}{a}=\frac{1}{abcd}(a^2cd+b^2da+c^2ab+d^2bc)=[/imath]
[imath]=\frac{1}{abcd}(a\cdot acd+b\cdot bda+c\cdot cab+d\cdot dbc)\leq[/imath]
[imath]\leq\frac{1}{abcd}(x\cdot xyz+y\cdot xyt+z\cdot xzt+t\cdot yzt)=[/imath]
[imath]=\frac{1}{abcd}(x^2yz+y^2xt+z^2xt+t^2yz)=\frac{1}{abcd}(xy+zt)(xz+yt)\leq[/imath]
[imath]\leq\frac{1}{abcd}\left(\frac{xy+zt+xz+yt}{2}\right)^2=[/imath]
[imath]=\frac{1}{4abcd}\left((x+t)(y+z)\right)^2\leq\frac{1}{4abcd}\left(\frac{x+y+z+t}{2}\right)^4=\frac{4}{abcd}.[/imath]
